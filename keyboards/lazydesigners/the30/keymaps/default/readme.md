```
,-------------------------------------------------.
| Q  | W  | E  | R  | T  | Y  | U  | I  | O  | P  |
|-------------------------------------------------|
| A  | S  | D  | F  | G  | H  | J  | K  | L  |ENT |
|-------------------------------------------------|
| Z  | X  | C  | V  | B  | N  | M  | ,  | .  | /  |
`-------------------------------------------------'
```
